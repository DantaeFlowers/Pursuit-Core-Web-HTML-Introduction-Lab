The Perfect PB&J

Description

The classic PB&J sandwich fun for the entire family

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • 1 jar grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slice of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the right side
  3. On the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. With a sharp knife carefully cut the sandwich in half
  6. Enjoy!
